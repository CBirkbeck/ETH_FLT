Modular forms

These are holomorphic functions \(f : \mathbb{H} \to \mathbb{C}, \) with some bounded coundition at infinity such that for some fixed \(k \in \mathbb{Z}\) and \(\Gamma \in \mathrm{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z}) \) we have \[\forall \left ( \begin{smallmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{smallmatrix} \right) \in \Gamma, \qquad f \left(\frac{az+b}{cz+d} \right) = (cz+d)^k f(z) \]

Algebra and Number theory in mathlib

I really have missed out lots of things, for example:

  • Category theory
  • Homological algebra
  • Lie Algebras
  • Riemann Zeta function, L-series
  • Quadratic reciprocity, Bernoulli numbers, Pell's equation

Algebra and Number theory in mathlib

We are also still missing lots of things! For example, for full FLT, we need:

  • More ring theory: Gorenstein rings
  • More modular form results: Hecke algebras*
  • Representation theory: (p-adic) Cyclotomic characters*
  • Lie algebra cohomology
* means I know some people as thinking about this, ask on Zulip if interested.
